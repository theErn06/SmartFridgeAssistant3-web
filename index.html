System Overview

Jarvis is an intelligent inventory management system that uses natural language processing to keep track of your groceries, prevent food waste, and automatically synchronize with your cloud dashboard.

Interaction Guide

  • 🗣️

    Voice Mode

    Simply say "Hey Jarvis" (or your configured wake word) followed by a natural command like "Add two apples" or "Remove the milk." The system uses Whisper STT to instantly transcribe your speech.

  • ⌨️

    Text Mode

    If you are testing the system on your laptop, switch to Text Mode to bypass the wake word. Type commands directly into the terminal prompt to manage the fridge database manually.

  • 🗑️

    Waste Analytics

    When you remove an item after its expiry date, Jarvis automatically flags it as waste. Ask Jarvis to "Summarize my waste for the week" to generate the reports shown in your Summary tab.

CAPSTONE PROJECT

Smart Refrigerator Assistant

(Project Jarvis)

Developed by Year 3 students from the Bachelor of Electrical & Electronics (E&E) program at TARUMT.

This system integrates advanced Edge-LLM technology, real-time voice recognition, and custom hardware to create a seamless, voice-activated smart kitchen experience.
